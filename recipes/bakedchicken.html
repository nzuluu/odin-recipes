Simple Baked Chicken Breasts

Image by valeria_aksakova on Freepik

Description

Only 5 Ingredients to make this tender, juicy and baked chicken breast.

Ingredients:
  • 2 tablespoons of olive Oil
  • 4 chicken Breasts
  • 1/2 teaspoon of salt
  • 1 pinch of creole seasoning
  • 1 tablespoon of chicken broth

Steps:

  1. Preheat oven to 400 degrees F

  2. Rub chicken breast with oil and sprinkle both sides with salt and creole seasoning, then place in the broiler pan

  3. Bake for 10 minutes, flip chicken after 10 and make sure the temp is 165 degrees F

  4. Remove chicken to a plate and pour the chicken broth into the pan and scrape the browned bits of the bottom to get the seasoning mixed in the broth

  5. Pour the pan sauce over the chicken and done.
