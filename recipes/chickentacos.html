Slow Cooker Chicken Tacos

Image by freepik

Description

Flavorful chicken tacos recipe using only 3 ingredients in a slow cooker. Combine the chicken into a tortillas for a tasty meal on any day.

Ingredients:
  • 1 cup chicken broth
  • 3 tablespoons taco seasoning mix
  • 1 pound skinless, boneless chicken breasts

Steps:

  1. Gather all ingredients

  2. Combine chicken broth and taco seasoning mix in a bowl

  3. Place chicken in a slow cooker and pour chicken broth mixture over chicken

  4. Cook on low for 6 to 8 hours then shred chicken with two forks

  5. Serve shredded chicken in warmed corn tortillas and top it with whatever you like
